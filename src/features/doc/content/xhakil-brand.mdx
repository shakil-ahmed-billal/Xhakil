## Brand History and Concept

The Xhakil brand signifies clean aesthetic, modern vibe-coding, and performant web development! The icons and logotypes represent a focused, forward-thinking approach. The dark base with solid letterforms offers excellent versatility for both light and dark-themed interfaces.

## Our Logos

Our brand consists of a standalone Mark (or icon) and a Logotype, which serve different purposes. 

### The Xhakil Mark

The Mark is a standalone "S" symbol inside a solid bounding box. It’s perfect for avatars, social media profiles, favicons, or anywhere vertical space is limited.

!["Xhakil Mark"](/shakil/shakil-mark.svg)

### The Xhakil Logotype (Wordmark)

The Logotype relies on our specific geometric typographic form, designed for readability and strong brand presence in UI headers, document footers, or marketing materials.

!["Xhakil Wordmark"](/shakil/shakil-icon.svg)
